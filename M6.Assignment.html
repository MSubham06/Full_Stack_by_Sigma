1. Hr Tag in HTML :

The < hr > tag in HTML, short for "horizontal rule", is used to create a horizontal line across a webpage.

It acts as a visual separator, dividing content sections or marking thematic breaks within a page. It's a self-closing tag, meaning it doesn't require a closing tag, and it's universally recognized by web browsers.
